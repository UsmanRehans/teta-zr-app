Our Story

💔

El Meshkleh (The Problem)

Beirut is FULL of incredible tetas — their kibbeh w their wara2 3enab could beat any fancy restaurant, wallah. Bas they have no way to share it beyond their building.

🍽️

El Wa2e3 (The Reality)

W meanwhile, ktir shabeb w banet — students, young professionals, expats — are dying for a real home-cooked meal. Stuck between overpriced restaurants w bland delivery apps.

❤️

El Hall (The Solution)

So we said yalla, let's fix this. Teta connects home cooks with hungry neighbors. No commissions. Bas neighbors feeding neighbors. Hek lazem tkun.
